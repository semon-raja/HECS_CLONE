Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) at HECS

At HECS, we believe progress is meaningful only when it uplifts communities and safeguards the environment. Beyond business, our CSR initiatives are designed to make a lasting impact where it matters most—safe water, education and environmental awareness.

CEO's Message

At HECS, Corporate Social Responsibility is not just an obligation — it is at the heart of who we are and what we stand for. Our journey in environmental services has always been rooted in a deeper purpose: to protect nature, uplift communities, and build a cleaner, healthier future.. As leaders in wastewater treatment, pollution prevention, and eco-conscious innovation, we use our expertise to safeguard natural resources, empower communities, and build a cleaner, healthier future. Our CSR efforts focus on water accessibility, sewage management, ecosystem restoration, and educational outreach, especially in underserved areas. Partnering with educational institutions, government bodies, and community organizations, we work to improve water quality, revive aquatic ecosystems, and promote circular water economy principles. These initiatives align with India's Sustainable Development Goals, particularly in clean water access, environmental protection, and holistic community well-being. HECS remains dedicated to driving meaningful change that uplifts communities and protects our planet.

Dr. JR Moses, CEO
Hubert Enviro Care Systems Ltd.

Strengthening Educational Infrastructure

Education is the cornerstone of empowerment. We work with schools—particularly in underserved areas—to improve learning environments and provide the resources children need to thrive. School Renovation & Upgrades: Enhancing classrooms, libraries, and science labs to create safe, inspiring spaces. Essential Facilities: Supplying desks, benches, and sanitation facilities for comfort and dignity in learning. Digital & Learning Tools: Supporting access to modern educational resources to bridge gaps and prepare students for tomorrow. By strengthening the backbone of education, HECS is helping shape confident, capable, and future-ready generations.

Our Commitment

CSR at HECS goes beyond obligation—it is a responsibility we embrace with purpose. By investing in education, sustainable water management, and environmental awareness, we are sowing the seeds of long-term community well-being and environmental protection. Together, we can create schools that nurture young minds, safeguard their health, and instill values that protect the planet for generations to come.

Environment Awareness & Training Programs

At the heart of HECS is a mission to nurture respect and responsibility towards the environment. Alongside infrastructure support, we actively engage schools in building eco-conscious habits. Workshops & Training Sessions – Introducing children to sustainability, waste management, and resource conservation. Eco-Clubs & Student Engagement – Encouraging young leaders to drive green initiatives within schools and communities. Hands-On Projects – Tree planting, waste segregation, and plastic-free campaigns that build responsibility through action. By instilling awareness at an early age, we empower students to become responsible custodians of the environment.

Safe Water in Schools: Treatment, Reuse & Sustainability

Access to safe water is more than a necessity—it is the foundation of health, hygiene, and dignity in schools. At HECS, we go beyond drinking water solutions to promote complete water cycle management for long-term sustainability. Water Treatment Systems: Installation of purification units to provide clean and safe drinking water for children. Sewage Treatment in Schools: Setting up compact sewage treatment plants (STPs) to ensure wastewater from toilets and wash areas is safely treated. Reuse of Treated Water: Recycling treated wastewater for flushing, gardening, and other non-potable applications, reducing dependence on freshwater sources. Rainwater Harvesting & Storage: Harnessing natural resources to strengthen water availability and resilience. Water Hygiene Awareness: Training students and staff on safe water practices and conservation. By integrating treatment and reuse, we ensure that schools not only have safe water today but also contribute to a sustainable water future.
